Wildfires in Australia

This visualisation shows us how much wildfire occurs daily in all areas of Australia, from 1st August 2019 to 31st August 2019. Firstly, we would be looking at a geographical map of Australia and locations of wildfire occurences.

Day vs Night

Now, we will look at the wildfire occurences during the day versus during the night. One of the main reasons of wildfire occurences is due to the high temperature, therefore daytime would occupy most of the occurences spotted.

Daily occurences

The graph to the right indicates how much wildfire occurences has been spotted daily. You can drag on the first graph(the graph with blue line) to choose your own time range for more detailed visualisation. The colour on the graph below it tells us how bright is the fire seen from satellite. The darker the colour, the brighter it is.

Temperature and brightness

Viewer tends to confuse between temperature and brightness. Here we show temperature and brightness of wildfire against time. We can observe from the graph that the trend lines are different so it does not correlate each other too much.
